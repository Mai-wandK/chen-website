Frequently Asked Questions

The Most Common Questions

Is there a fee for late cancellations or no-shows?



Yes, if you cancel your appointment less than 6 hours in advance or miss your appointment without notifying us, there is a $30 fee. We are a busy clinic and do not double-book clients. Each time slot is specifically reserved for you, and if you do not show up, that time is wasted. For this reason, we are strict with our cancellation policy.

Are you a licensed acupuncturist so I can claim from my insurance company?



Yes, the acupuncturist in our clinic is licensed.

How much does an acupuncture session cost and how long for each session?



We charge $75 per session and each session is around 45 minutes.

Can you direct bill to my insurance?



Yes, we can direct bill to most of the insurance companies like Canada life, ClaimSecure, Equitable, Industrial Alliance, Manulife, Sunlife and more!

What is acupuncture and how does it work?



Acupuncture is a key component of traditional Chinese medicine. It has been practiced in China for more than 3000 years. Acupuncture treats health conditions by balancing energy flow known as chi.

How long does acupuncture take to work?



For internal issues you need multiple sessions to see results, for pain most likely you will see results after the first session.

What does acupuncture treat?



Most health issues can be treated or benefited by acupuncture, all kinds of body pain, digestion issues, anxiety and stress, Bell’s palsy, stroke recovery, fertility and much more.

I’m so afraid of needles, can I have treatment without needles?



Yes, we can use acupressure instead but it's less productive.

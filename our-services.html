What are your fees?

Each treatment session is priced at $75 and lasts about 45 minutes. A standalone Meridian checkup costs $20, but this fee is waived if you receive treatment, reducing your cost to $75. For house calls, we charge the same $75 in Brampton, but additional fees may apply for areas like Mississauga, Georgetown, and Caledon to cover mileage.

02

What if I don’t feel better after treatment?

If your health concern is pain, you can expect to see immediate improvement after your first treatment; if not, we’ll provide a full refund. While our treatments are effective for a range of issues, the quick results for pain make it easy to evaluate their effectiveness right away.

03

What common things are treated with acupuncture?

Acupuncture is effective for conditions such as back pain, frozen shoulder, migraines, tennis elbow, insomnia, sciatica, anxiety, stress, diabetes, arthritis, infertility, and constipation.
